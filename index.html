About Us

Sip with Style:

OUR TUMBLERS ARE THE FUTURE

Our business is on a mission to reduce the use of plastic bottles by collaborating with Kpop idols to promote our eco-friendly tumblers. With a stylish and affordable design, our tumblers are the perfect alternative to plastic bottles.

By choosing to sip sustainably with our tumblers, you can show your support for a cleaner and more sustainable planet. Our collaboration with Kpop idols is just one way we are inspiring others to make small changes in their daily lives for a brighter future. Join us in our mission to reduce plastic waste and make our tumblers the future of hydration.
